Privacy Policy

Who We Are

DeveLoopIt is a software development company dedicated to creating innovative solutions. Our website address is: https://DeveLoopIt.net

Notice

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services. By using our website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and provide essential functionality. When you visit our website, you have the option to accept or reject non-essential cookies through our cookie banner.

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze how you use our website
  • Improve our website's performance and user experience
  • Provide personalized content and advertisements

How We Collect Your Data

We collect information through:

  • Newsletter Subscription: When you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address to send you updates, news, and exclusive offers.
  • Project Discussion Form: When you use our "Discuss Project" form, we collect information such as your name, email address, and project details to better understand your requirements and provide appropriate services.
  • Website Usage: We collect anonymous data about how you interact with our website to improve our services.

How Long We Retain Your Data

For newsletter subscribers, we retain your email address until you choose to unsubscribe. You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in our emails.

For project discussion submissions, we retain your information for as long as necessary to process your request and provide our services. You can request the deletion of your data at any time.

Website administrators can see and manage this information to provide better service and support.

What Rights You Have Over Your Data

If you have subscribed to our newsletter or submitted a project discussion form, you can:

  • Request to receive an exported file of the personal data we hold about you
  • Request that we erase any personal data we hold about you
  • Unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time
  • Update your preferences regarding cookie usage

This does not include any data we are obliged to keep for administrative, legal, or security purposes.

Where We Send Your Data

Visitor comments may be checked through an automated spam detection service. We may also share your information with:

  • Service providers who assist in operating our website
  • Analytics providers to help us understand how our website is used
  • Law enforcement when required by law
